About Animal Health Ireland

About AHI

AHI provides benefits to livestock producers and processors by providing the knowledge, education and coordination required to establish effective control programmes for non-regulated diseases of livestock.

Stakeholders

AHI have a number of Stakeholder across the various sectors inlcuding Dairy and Beef processors, Farmer's Organisations and Livestock, AI Companies, Government and State Agencies and Professional/Advisory/Supoort Service.

Directors

Currently six members sit on the Board of Directors for Animal Health Ireland. The members come from both industry and farmer backgrounds.

Management Team

Along with the CEO we also have 5 Progarmmes Managers, 3 Senior staff memebers and 5 members of our administrative team.

Technical Working Groups

The Technical Working Groups (TWGs) comprise experts and experienced practitioners from a variety of fields who are tasked with drawing up factual resources, the development of decision-making tools, and the identification of areas for further Research and Development.

Implementation Groups

In conjunction with the relevant Technical Working Group, the Implementation Groups (IGs) are responsible for the development, implementation and monitoring of major animal health programmes.
